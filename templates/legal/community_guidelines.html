Community Guidelines

Building a respectful, inclusive community together — one interaction at a time.

Welcome to Campus Connect

Campus Connect is a safe, verified network built exclusively for students and staff at accredited institutions. These Community Guidelines help us maintain a respectful, inclusive space where everyone can connect, collaborate, and thrive.

We're not just about rules — we're about building community values that make Campus Connect a better place for everyone.

Foundation

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by these four principles.

Respect

We treat each other with dignity and kindness. Respectful disagreement is welcome; personal attacks are not.

Authenticity

Be yourself and be genuine. Campus Connect is about building real community with real people.

Inclusion

Everyone belongs here. We celebrate diversity and create space for all backgrounds, identities, and experiences.

Safety

Everyone deserves to feel safe online. Zero tolerance for harassment, threats, or harmful behavior.

✓
Expected

Behavior We Encourage

Be Kind and Supportive

Uplift others. Celebrate achievements, offer encouragement, and provide support.

Listen and Learn

Be open to different perspectives. Listen to understand, not to respond.

Share Constructively

Post content that adds value — helpful info, thoughtful questions, or genuine conversation.

Respect Privacy

Ask before sharing someone's story, information, or photo.

Look Out for Each Other

See something concerning? Report it. We all keep Campus Connect safe together.

✗
Prohibited

Behavior That Harms

Harassment or Bullying

No targeting, insulting, or threatening. Including cyberbullying and coordinated attacks.

Hate Speech or Discrimination

Zero tolerance for attacks based on identity — race, gender, orientation, religion, disability.

Impersonation or Deception

One authentic identity per person. Fake accounts will be removed.

Unwanted Sexual Content

No sexually explicit content, non-consensual images, or sexual harassment.

Privacy Violations

No doxxing or sharing others' private information without consent.

Spam or Manipulation

No excessive posting, fake engagement, or coordinated inauthentic behavior.

Appeals Process

  • 2. Appeal within 30 days via email.
  • 3. Moderation team review.
  • 4. Final decision within 30 days.
