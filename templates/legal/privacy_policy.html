Data Protection

Privacy Policy

We care about your data privacy and give you full control over your information.

Effective: January 1, 2026  ·  Updated: March 7, 2026

Portfolio Disclaimer: Campus Connect is a demonstration project and not a commercial service. The policies described here illustrate typical platform practices.

Campus Connect respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information in a transparent, easy-to-understand manner.

Section 1

Information We Collect

Account Details

Full name, email, password (hashed), date of birth, university, and grade level provided during registration.

Platform Activity

Posts, comments, connections, group memberships, event RSVPs, and content you voluntarily publish.

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, operating system, and automatically collected system logs.

Tracking & Cookies

Essential session tokens and preferences that keep you logged in and remember your settings.

Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide and improve Campus Connect:

  • 1Provide Services: Create and manage your account, enable posts, deliver messages.
  • 2Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns to identify improvements and fix bugs.
  • 3Safety & Security: Detect fraud, prevent hacking attempts, investigate reports.
  • 4Communication: Account notifications, support responses, security alerts.
  • 5Research: Anonymized, aggregate statistics to understand user behavior.
Section 3

How We Share Your Data

🚫

We NEVER Sell Your Data

Data is NEVER sold to third parties
Data is NEVER shared with marketers
Data is NEVER rented to advertisers

Section 4

Your Privacy Rights

You maintain ultimate authority over your digital footprint.

1 Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold. Provided within 30 days.

2 Right to Correct

Update or correct your personal information instantly via Edit Profile.

3 Right to Erasure

Request complete deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting the site administrator.

4 Right to Restrict

Limit how we process your sensitive data temporarily or permanently.

5 Right to Object

Opt-out of certain processing, communications, and analytics.

6 Data Portability

Request your data summary by contacting the project administrator.

Section 6

Data Security Measures

Your platform security is backed by industry-leading measures:

  • Encryption: 256-bit SSL/TLS in transit and AES-256 at rest.
  • Access Control: Restrictive access models and strict session management.
  • Cloud Defenses: WAF and real-time vulnerability scanning.
  • Incident Response: Affected users notified within 30 days of any verified breach.
