FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

You can reset your password by going to the login page and clicking "Forgot Password". You will need access to your verified college email to receive the secure reset link.

New student accounts pre-created by the university administration remain in a "pending" state until you log in for the first time, change your temporary password, and complete your profile setup.

Your primary email is tied to your university enrollment and cannot be changed directly. If your institution has issued you a new email, contact your campus administrator to update it in the system.

Currently, only verified administrators and registered club representatives can create official campus events. Students can browse, register their interest, and RSVP to these events.

Open the event details page and click the "Register" or "RSVP" button. You'll receive a confirmation email and the event will appear in your personal calendar.

Yes! Enable event notifications in Settings → Notifications. You can choose to be notified about all campus events or only events from clubs and categories you follow.

At this time, posts cannot be edited once published to maintain platform transparency and safety. You can delete your post and create a new one if you made a mistake.

The campus feed is organized into categories including Announcements, Academics, Lost & Found, Buy & Sell, Clubs, and General Discussion. You can filter by category using the tabs at the top of the feed page.

All posts go through automated content filtering. Flagged content is reviewed by campus moderators. Users can also report posts using the three-dot menu. Spam, hate speech, and explicit content are removed immediately.

By default, your posts are visible to all verified students in your campus. When creating a post, you can choose visibility: "Campus-wide", "My Connections", or "Branch Only".

Use the "Connections" tab to search by name, branch, or batch. Send a connection request, and once accepted, you can chat and share content directly with each other.

Yes. Visit the person's profile and select "Remove Connection" or "Block User" from the options menu. Blocked users cannot see your profile, send you messages, or view your posts.

There is no hard limit on the number of connections. However, to prevent spam, you can send a maximum of 50 connection requests per day. Pending requests expire after 30 days.

You can send direct messages to anyone in your connections list. Messages support text, images, and file attachments up to 10 MB. All messages are encrypted and only visible to the sender and recipient.

Violations may result in a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban depending on the severity. Abusive content, spam, and harassment are taken very seriously.

Prohibited content includes hate speech, bullying, explicit material, misinformation, spam, unauthorized advertisements, and any content that violates your institution's code of conduct.

Submit an appeal through the Contact Support page. Include your username and a description. Appeals are typically reviewed within 3-5 business days.

Campus Connect follows industry-standard data protection practices. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, never sold to third parties. You may request an export or deletion of your data anytime by contacting the project administrator.
