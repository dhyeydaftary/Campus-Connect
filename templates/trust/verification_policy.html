Trust & Safety

Verification Policy

How we maintain a trusted, exclusive, and troll-free environment for the campus community.

Campus Connect is designed to be a safe space exclusively for students, faculty, and staff of recognized institutions. To achieve this, we enforce a strict verification process. Unverified users cannot access the feed, join events, or interact with other members.

Process

The Verification Process

1

Institutional Registration

You must register using an official college or university email address (e.g., @university.edu or @student.college.ac.in). Personal emails like Gmail or Yahoo are strictly prohibited.

2

Email Confirmation

A secure, time-sensitive verification link is sent to the institutional email. The account remains locked in a pending state until this link is clicked, proving ownership of the inbox.

3

Profile Moderation

Even after email verification, profiles with inappropriate names or violating our real-name policy may be flagged by the community and subject to review or suspension by administration.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Currently, Campus Connect operates strictly on an institutional email basis. If your institution does not provide student emails, your campus administration must reach out to us to establish a custom verification pipeline using student ID numbers.

Alumni retain read-only access to their specific campus network to preserve connections, assuming their institutional inbox forwards or remains active. Posting privileges may be restricted to "Alumni" specific boards depending on your institution's configuration.

For security purposes, we cannot manually bypass the email verification requirement. If you lose access to the email linked to your account, you must contact your university's IT department to restore access to the inbox. Otherwise, the account will be inaccessible.
