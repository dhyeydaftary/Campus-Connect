At Campus Connect, we believe that a safe network starts with respecting your privacy. We strictly limit the data we collect to what is absolutely necessary to verify your identity and power your campus experience. We do not sell your personal data to advertisers.

1. Information Collection

We collect information directly from you when you register and interact with the platform:

Identity Data: Full name, institutional email address, graduation year, and academic major.
Profile Data: Avatar images, bio, skills, and links you voluntarily add to your public profile.
Content Data: Posts, comments, messages, and event RSVPs generated by your activity.
Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and operating system used for security auditing and session management.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly to provide, maintain, and improve the Campus Connect service:

  • To verify your eligibility to access the institutional network.
  • To display your profile to other verified members of your specific campus.
  • To deliver notifications regarding events, mentions, or administrative alerts.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and enforce our Community Guidelines.

3. Information Sharing

We operate on a principle of least privilege regarding external sharing. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Law Enforcement / Institutional Administration

We may disclose information if legally required by a subpoena, court order, or formal request from your University's disciplinary board evaluating severe breaches of conduct (e.g., threats of violence).

Aside from legal compliance, your data is visible only to other verified members of your isolated campus network.

4. Data Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are cryptographically hashed using bcrypt. Production databases are isolated behind strict firewalls, accessible only by core engineering staff on a need-to-know basis.

5. Your Data Rights

You maintain control over your personal information:

Right to Rectification

Edit your profile information, posts, and comments at any time through the platform.

Right to Erasure

Request account and associated data deletion by contacting the site administrator. Deletions are processed within 30 days.

Right to Access

Request a summary of personal data tied to your account by contacting the site administrator.
